Request Account Deletion

If you no longer wish to use Laya, you can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated personal data.

What gets deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, bio, location).
  • All uploaded photos.
  • Your chat history and matches.
  • Your authentication data.

To request deletion, please send an email to our support team from the email address associated with your Laya account.
