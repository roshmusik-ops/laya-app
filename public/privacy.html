Privacy Policy for Laya

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

Welcome to Laya! Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Laya mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for Laya, we collect personal information including but not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Profile details such as age, gender, photos, and location.
  • Usage data (swipes, matches, and app activity).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide the core functionalities of the Laya app, including:

  • Matching you with compatible users based on your preferences.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your profile information is only visible to other users within the Laya app as per your privacy settings. We may share anonymized data with third-party analytics services to improve the app.

We implement industry-standard encryption to protect your data. All chat logs and video/audio calls are encrypted and routed securely.
